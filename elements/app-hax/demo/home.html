What is HAX?

HAX is a paradigm shift for web publishing. It puts advanced authoring capabilities in the hands of instructional designers, instructors, and students in a portable format that lives for ever.

Why HAX?

  • Simplicity - Simple Add, Make, Find system to ensure ease of use regardless of element being created
  • Sustainability - Writes HTML, a forever format that is platform agnostic!
  • Portability - HAX ultimately writes all files to a simple, human readable, download as a zip and send to another person style format. No databases, just pure HTML via web components.
  • Community - We're a diverse network of educators, students and staff driving requirements
  • Extensibility - We're adding new widgets, integrations and features to HAX all the time!
  • Remixable - HAX writes to a "flat file" format, meaning all files live in an easy to read folder structure. You can download it as a zip, clone spaces, and share them with ease in and out of Penn state.
  • Accessible - HAX elements and themes have been vetted by accessibility experts and the platform itself prevents common accessibility mistakes from being possible.
  • Stylish - HAX themes are decoupled from designed assets. Meaning if you change your mind and want to teach using a blog style courses as opposed to a traditional online course theme, no problem. Switch themes with the click of a button but content and structure maintain accuracy.
