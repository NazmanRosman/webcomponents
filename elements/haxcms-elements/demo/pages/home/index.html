What Is OER?

Open Edcuational Resources are freely accessible, openly licensed text, media, and other digital assets that are useful for teaching, learning, and research. Working with the talented faculty at our college, our office have been able to create several OER courses to share. Our intent is to provide resources that anyone can use, improve and redistribute.

Our OER Courses

How do I use these courses?

These courses are hosted on publicly available domains. You can link to the homepage of a course or link to specific modules within a course. Visit our how-to page for further instruction.
