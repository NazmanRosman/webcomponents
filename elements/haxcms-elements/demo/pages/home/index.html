What Is OER?

Open Edcuational Resources are freely accessible, openly licensed text, media, and other digital assets that are useful for teaching, learning, and research. Working with the talented faculty at our college, our office have been able to create several OER courses to share. Our intent is to provide resources that anyone can use, improve and redistribute.

Best Practices: Five Tips

Broadly speaking, these are the most important design considerations when creating instructional video:

  • Keep videos brief and focused on learning goals
  • Use audio and visual elements appropriately; make them complementary, not redundant
  • Use signaling to highlight important ideas or concepts
  • Use a conversational and enthusiastic narration style
  • Take an active learning approach: Associate your video with guiding questions, interactive elements, or assignments

But there is more! How do you do this? Part of the answer is process.  Read on to learn a three-phase process you can follow in twelve clearly explained steps.
